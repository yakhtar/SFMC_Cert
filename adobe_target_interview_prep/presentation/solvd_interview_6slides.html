üîß Technical Implementation

Adobe Target Configuration:
  • Activity Type: Multivariate Test
  • Combinations: 12 strategic selections (from 81 possible)

  • Sample Size: 15,000 users per combination

üí° MVT Advantage Over Sequential A/B Testing

Interaction Effects: MVT reveals how elements work together (e.g., time-estimated progress + value-driven CTA might perform better than either alone). This helps us find optimal combinations faster than testing elements individually over months.
