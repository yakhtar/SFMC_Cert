  • Option C: SSL Certificates secure web connections but are not used for email authentication. They don't help with email deliverability or provide the sender verification that ISPs require for inbox placement.
  • Option D: MX Records route incoming email to the correct mail servers but don't provide any authentication or anti-spoofing protection. They're essential for receiving email but don't impact the deliverability of outbound campaigns.

Key Learning Points:

  • DMARC completes the email authentication trinity (SPF + DKIM + DMARC)
  • Major ISPs require DMARC alignment for optimal deliverability
  • DMARC provides both authentication and valuable reporting capabilities
